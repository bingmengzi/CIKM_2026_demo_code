Key Concepts

  • 🔴 Hour Hand: Short and thick, moves slowly, indicates "hours".
  • 🔵 Minute Hand: Thin and long, moves faster, indicates "minutes".
  • ⚡ When the minute hand completes one full circle (60 min), the hour hand moves one large mark (1 hour).
