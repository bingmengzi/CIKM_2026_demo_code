💡 Key takeaways

🔍 Idea

Use a cut-and-rearrange method to turn a parallelogram into a rectangle. The shape changes, but the area stays the same.

📏 Key elements

A parallelogram’s base and height correspond to a rectangle’s length and width.

✨ Area formula

A = base × height (not the slanted side!)

🎯 Note

The height must be perpendicular to the base, not the slanted side.
