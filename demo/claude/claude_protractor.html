📐 Interactive Protractor

Learn how to use a protractor to measure angles

Mode: Free draw
Current angle: 0°

📖 How to use

1️⃣ Click three times on the canvas to draw an angle (vertex → ray 1 → ray 2)
2️⃣ Click “Show protractor”
3️⃣ Drag the protractor so its center aligns with the angle’s vertex
4️⃣ Rotate the protractor (use the rotate buttons) so the 0° baseline aligns with one ray
5️⃣ Read the degree value where the other ray meets the scale
